About US

At Blueberry Family Salon, we believe style is more than appearance — it is confidence, personality, and self-expression.

Founded in 2026, our salon was created to offer a modern, premium, and welcoming experience for men, women, and families. From precision haircuts and professional grooming to hair treatments, styling, and beauty care, every service is delivered with attention to detail and care.

Our team combines creativity, professionalism, and modern trends to help every client look and feel their best. With a clean contemporary atmosphere and personalized service, Blueberry Family Salon is designed to be a place where comfort meets elegance.

We are committed to quality, hygiene, customer satisfaction, and creating styles that suit your individuality.
